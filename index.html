About us

I am a post-graduate and currently I am working as a professor in one of the reputed University in Chennai. Even though I was happy with my profession and life, somewhere I had a feeling that I was not satisfied with what I am doing and started my quest on next steps on my life journey.


My ancestors and my fore fathers were involved in Agriculture,Dairy and Poultry. My family, since I was a child involved in Dairy business, supplying milk and milk products to nearby localities.


I realized that Dairy is in my blood, I wished to take the business to the next level and thus established “Mahalakshmi Dairy Farm” currently located in OMR Padur, Chennai. Initially I was taking the business only on weekends, But due to customer support and their motivation now I have started taking this on week days as well but on half time basis.


This is just a start up and with your blessings and wishes, I hope I will satisfy my customers delivering quality products with at most sincerity and commitment.
